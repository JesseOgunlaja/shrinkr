Privacy Policy

Effective as of April 17, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Shrinkr collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our service.

1. Data Controller

The data controller is Shrinkr. For privacy-related inquiries, you can contact us at support@shrinkr.dev.

2. Data We Collect

We collect the following personal data:

  • Email address
  • Clerk-generated user ID
  • First and last name (only when using Google OAuth via Clerk, not stored by us)

Cookies are used only for authentication purposes.

We use Vercel Analytics to collect anonymous usage data to help us improve the service.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely for account management. We do not send marketing emails or newsletters.

4. Third-Party Services

We rely on the following third-party services:

  • Clerk: For authentication and user management
  • Upstash: For database storage

We do not share your data with any third parties beyond these services.

5. International Data Transfers

We do not knowingly transfer data outside the UK or EEA.

6. Data Retention

We retain user data indefinitely unless the user deletes their account via the settings page.

7. Security

We use Clerk for secure authentication. All user accounts are verified through email before being activated.

8. Your Rights

As a user, you have the right to access and delete your data. You can view and manage your data within the settings page or request assistance by contacting us at support@shrinkr.dev.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy at any time. Users are responsible for reviewing this page periodically to stay informed of any changes.

By using Shrinkr, you agree to this Privacy Policy.
